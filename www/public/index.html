FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you still have any questions? Don’t worry, we have answers for you!

How much should I pay?
Prices are set by individual TaBee partners, but you can expect the prices to be lower than what most junk removal or shredding companies would cost. You will be able to see your individual price after you have selected a service and entered your pickup address, before you submit the request.
When should I expect a TaBee partner to come?
Our TaBee partners set the time (which should work for both parties), and which could even make the service cheaper.
What kind of junk does TaBee take?
When you fill out the Junk Removal request form, you will be able to see a full list of stuff that our TaBee partners can pick up, as well as the service cost.
Who is the TaBee app made for?
The TaBee app is made for anyone who wishes to recycle, and to leave less of an impact on our planet and our environment. We invite partners to work with us as a team, providing junk removal, shredding, and donation pickup services for everyone who wishes to recycle their stuff, all through one app. If you would like to become a TaBee partner, simply visit the Partner website.
