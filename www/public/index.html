FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Still got questions? No worries, we have answers!

How much should I pay?
TaBee partners set their prices which are usually lower than junk removal or shredding companies offer. Ones you have pointed the service and a pickup address you will be able to see the cost before the request submission.
When should I expect a TaBee partner to come?
TaBee partners set the time which would work for both of you and could make the service cheaper.
What kind of junk can you take out?
While filling in the junk removal request, you will be able to see the full list of stuff that TaBee partners can pick up and the service cost.
Who is the TaBee app made for?
For everyone who cares about the future generations and intend to decrease the amount of waste on the Earth. We also invite partners to work as a team in providing junk removal, shredding, donation pickup services through the app. To find out more please, see the Partner website.
