Still got questions? No worries, we have answers!

What kind of recyclables does Mobile Recycling Systems Collect?
Just about any you can think of! Plastic, glass, or aluminum beverage bottles, soda cans, all types of plastics, cartons, office paper, envelopes, newspapers, magazines, cellophane, scrap metal, and even cigarette butts! These are all things that get tossed in trash cans that end up in landfills.
Who is this App Made For?
It’s made for everyone! Anyone that wants to help the environment in their own way. Everyday people like you, your friends, and your neighbors can use our system to make it simpler to recycle!
How Do I Know How Much I Get Paid?
All you have to do is collect your recyclables, and when you have them ready to go, just log onto the app and let us know you are ready for us to come collect it. When our driver partner arrives at your house, they will weigh your recyclables on the scales that are equipped in their truck. You can get paid one of two methods that are up to you--good old-fashioned cash, or bank transfer straight to your account!
